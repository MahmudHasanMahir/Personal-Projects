# 200kW Solar Panel Support Structure — Curtin University

**Tools:** Autodesk Revit, Structural Steel Detailing

## Overview
Structural design and detailing of a steel portal frame support structure for a 200kW solar panel array, modelled in Autodesk Revit. The project covers general arrangement, elevations, and full connection detailing.

## Scope of Work
- Modelled a steel portal frame with high column, low column, rafter and base plate elements to achieve the correct panel tilt angle.
- Produced a full structural drawing set: general arrangement plans (sheet S.1), elevations (sheet S.2), and 1:5 scale connection details (sheet S.3).
- Detailed bolted connections specifying M16 Grade 8.8 bolts across rafter-to-purlin, column-to-rafter, and base plate connections.
- Specified 0.35 m × 0.35 m reinforced concrete footings to support column base loads.
- Coordinated column heights (0.4 m low column, 1.1 m high column) and a 0.1 m low haunch to achieve the required solar panel tilt.
