# Site Terrain & Civil Site Evaluation — Site 44

**Tools:** Autodesk Civil 3D, Contour Mapping

## Overview
A civil site evaluation of existing ground contours to assess site topography for a proposed multi-pad development.

## Scope of Work
- Interpreted existing ground contour data at 0.5 m intervals across a local survey grid (Easting/Northing coordinates).
- Assessed site topography across three proposed development pads (Pad A, Pad B, Pad C).
- Analysed contour patterns to identify high/low points and likely drainage paths, informing recommendations for pad placement, access routing, and earthworks/grading strategy.
- Presented findings as a scaled engineering site plan to support practical, commercially viable site development decisions.
