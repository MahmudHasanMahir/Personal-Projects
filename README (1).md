# Multi-Storey Carpark Structural Model

**Tools:** Autodesk Revit, Building Information Modelling (BIM)

## Overview
A detailed 3D Building Information Model (BIM) of a multi-storey carpark structure, developed in Autodesk Revit. The model includes four structural levels plus a fully framed roof structure.

## Scope of Work
- Modelled structural columns, beams and floor slabs across four levels: Ground Floor (0 mm), First Floor (5,000 mm), Second Floor (9,000 mm) and Third Floor (13,000 mm).
- Designed and modelled a fully framed roof structure at 17,000 mm, including roof beams and bracing.
- Maintained accurate level coordination and structural connectivity between all floors and the roof framing.
- Produced 3D structural visualisations to communicate design intent and support clash detection between structural elements.
